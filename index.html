About this project

Canon Law 1251: "Abstinence from meat, or from some other food as determined by the Episcopal Conference, is to be observed on all Fridays, UNLESS a solemnity should fall on a Friday. Abstinence and fasting are to be observed on Ash Wednesday and Good Friday."

Meat Fridays are special Fridays where the abstinence from meat is dispensed as a solemnity falls on that same Friday. This website was created for Catholics to note down any and all Meat Fridays in a year, so that we may not miss a chance to feast on a Friday!

Simply enter in the Easter Date, click submit, and it will calculate all Meat Fridays in that year! Happy feasting, and Ave Maria!
